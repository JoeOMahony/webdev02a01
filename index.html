Data Manipulation

The data manipulation page provides a fully functional computer management system that is searchable by any value, or partial value, of the data stored. For instance, you can search by a manufacturer like Apple or HP, or by the type of computing device, or even by a specific part of the specifications, e.g., entering 256GB will return any computing device with storage or even RAM (server) of 256GB.
